Types of Galaxies

Galaxies are enormous collections of stars, planets, gas, and dark matter. Here are the main types:

  • Spiral Galaxies: Have beautiful spiral arms, like the Milky Way.
  • Elliptical Galaxies: Shaped like ellipsoids, often containing older stars.
  • Irregular Galaxies: No distinct shape, often formed by galactic collisions.
