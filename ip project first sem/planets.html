Our Solar System

The solar system contains eight unique planets. Each has distinct features:

  • Mercury: The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.
  • Venus: Known as Earth's twin because of its similar size, but extremely hot.
  • Earth: The only planet known to support life.
  • Mars: The red planet with the tallest volcano in the solar system.
  • Jupiter: The largest planet with a giant storm called the Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn: Famous for its beautiful ring system.
  • Uranus: An ice giant that rotates on its side.
  • Neptune: The farthest planet, known for its deep blue color and strong winds.
